cam_setup#

cam_setup(**kwargs)[source]#

Setup to compute a normalized cross-correlation between a target f and a template g over their means. In practice this can be expressed like the cross-correlation CORR defined in corr_scoring(), so that:

See also

corr_scoring()
tme.matching_optimization.NormalizedCrossCorrelationMean

Notes

\[\text{CORR}(f-\overline{f}, g - \overline{g})\]

Where

\[\overline{f}, \overline{g}\]

are the mean of f and g respectively.

References

[1]

J. P. Lewis, “Fast Normalized Cross-Correlation”, Industrial Light and Magic.
